<p>Iran reaffirms it will not pursue nuclear weapons, and the two sides agree to down-blend stockpiled enriched material on-site under IAEA supervision, with enrichment needs addressed in the final deal. The MOU sets a maximum 60-day timeline—extendable by mutual consent—for negotiating and concluding the final deal, and the final accord will be endorsed by a binding UN Security Council resolution.</p>
