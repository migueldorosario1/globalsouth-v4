<p>UN Special Rapporteur Francesca Albanese has traced Israeli methods of sexual torture against Palestinians directly to British Mandate emergency regulations. The broader colonial lineage, as detailed in the source, also includes France's counterinsurgency doctrine in Algeria. Survivor Rasmea Odeh’s testimony of rape with a stick and electric shocks in 1969, she says, is not an aberration but part of an inherited imperial system designed to humiliate and control.</p>
