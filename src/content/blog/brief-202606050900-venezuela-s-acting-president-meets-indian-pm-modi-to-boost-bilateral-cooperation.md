<p>Venezuela’s acting president, Delcy Rodríguez, met Indian prime minister Narendra Modi at Hyderabad House in New Delhi on Thursday to discuss expanding bilateral ties in strategic sectors. The talks, held during an official working visit, covered transport, health, energy complementarity, agriculture, science and technology.</p>
