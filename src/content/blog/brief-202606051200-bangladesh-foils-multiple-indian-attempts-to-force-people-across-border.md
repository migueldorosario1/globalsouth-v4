<p>Dhaka has insisted that any Bangladeshi nationals must be returned through formal legal and diplomatic channels rather than informal “push-ins” that bypass verification and repatriation procedures. “No individual or group will be allowed to enter Bangladesh illegally through the border,” the BGB said, adding that violations of international border management norms would be “strictly resisted.” Bangladesh’s border forces last month intensified patrols and launched public awareness campaigns along parts of the 4,000-kilometre frontier.</p>
