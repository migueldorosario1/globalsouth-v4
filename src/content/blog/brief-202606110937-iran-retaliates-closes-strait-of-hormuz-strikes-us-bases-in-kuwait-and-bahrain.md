<p>On 11 June 2026, Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guards Corps (IRGC) said it had launched retaliatory strikes against US forces in the region, specifically naming bases in Kuwait and Bahrain, according to Iranian news reports. Earlier, the US military’s Central Command (CENTCOM) carried out strikes “against multiple targets in Iran”, which it described as “self-defense” and a response to “Iran’s unwarranted and continued aggression”. Live updates also reported that Iran ordered the complete closure of the Strait of Hormuz to all vessels.</p>
