<p>Under the MOU, the US will immediately begin lifting its naval blockade and fully end it within 30 days, gradually restoring commercial vessel traffic. Iran will facilitate safe passage for commercial ships through the Strait of Hormuz at no charge for 60 days, while coordinating with Oman on long-term maritime administration in line with international law. Washington also undertakes to terminate all US and UN sanctions on an agreed schedule, make frozen Iranian assets fully available upon implementation of the MOU for use by the Central Bank of Iran, and immediately issue Treasury waivers for Iranian crude oil exports and associated banking, insurance, and transportation services.</p>
