<p>The Trump administration has announced a new US-mediated ceasefire agreement between Israel and Lebanon, but the initiative was immediately rejected by Hezbollah leader Naim Qassem, who called it a “surrender and defeat”. Israeli Defence Minister Israel Katz insisted military operations would continue regardless of the deal, which Lebanese President Joseph Aoun said would enter force within 24 hours of approval by all parties. The accord was brokered through Lebanese government diplomats without Hezbollah’s participation.</p>
